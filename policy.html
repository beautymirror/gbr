Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 22, 2024

Digital Global Solutions LLP ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Global Beauty Rank website (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

1. Information We Collect

We are committed to collecting minimal data necessary to provide and improve our Service. The information we collect is as follows:

  • Photographs: You provide a photograph for analysis. We do not store your photographs. The image is sent to our AI model for processing and is immediately and permanently deleted after the analysis is complete.
  • User-Provided Data: We collect your first name, gender, age range, country, and optionally, your profession.
  • Service-Generated Data: We generate and store the beauty score associated with your provided information.

We explicitly do not collect or store any payment card information. All payment transactions are handled by our third-party processor, Stripe.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our Service, specifically to generate your beauty score.
  • To create anonymized, aggregated statistical data for our public rankings (e.g., "Average score in Canada," "Top score for men").
  • To improve the accuracy and performance of our AI model.

Your personal data is never sold to third parties.

3. Data Storage and Security

The limited data we retain (name, gender, age range, country, profession, and score) is stored on secure servers. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party service providers:

  • Stripe: To process payments securely. We do not have access to your payment card details. We encourage you to review Stripe's Privacy Policy.

5. Your Data Rights

You have the right to request the deletion of your personal information (name, gender, age range, country, profession, and score) from our database. To do so, please contact us at the email address provided below.
